# **App Name**: AmigosIAS Hub

## Core Features:

- Course Listings: Display detailed information about available courses such as Integrated Prelims cum Mains, Prelims Value Addition Program, Mains Value Addition Program, Interview Guidance Program, including schedules, fees, and faculty.
- Success Stories: Showcase student testimonials and success stories with images and write-ups.
- Contact Form: Enable potential students to contact the academy through a submission form with fields for name, email, phone number, and message.
- AI Study Planner Tool: Allow students to input study hours and exam goals, use AI tool to generates a personalized daily study plan, adjusting difficulty and subject matter based on student preferences and performance.The AI tool uses reasoning to decide when or if to incorporate some piece of information in its output.
- Faculty Profiles: Introduce the faculty members with their qualifications, experience, and specializations to build trust and credibility.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep orange (#FF4500) to convey wisdom and intellect, echoing a sophisticated learning atmosphere.
- Background color: Soft peach (#FFE5B4), a desaturated version of orange, provides a calming, focused environment conducive to studying.
- Accent color: Coral (#FF8000), slightly brighter than the primary, draws attention to important information and calls-to-action.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif, to convey techy look
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif, for clear and readable paragraphs.
- Modern, minimalist icons for course categories and site navigation.
- Clean, grid-based layout with ample whitespace for easy content scanning.
- Subtle transitions and animations for enhanced user engagement.
